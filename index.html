ℹ️ How to Play

🎯 Goal: Drag (or tap) each rubbish item into the correct bin before time runs out.


GREEN Organics – food scraps & garden waste
YELLOW Recycling – clean plastics (1,2,5), cans, paper & cardboard
ORANGE Resource Recovery – hazardous items (batteries, chemicals, e-waste)
RED General Waste – non-recyclables, soft plastics, aerosols
BLUE Glass – clean clear glass bottles & jars (no lids!)


⚡ Actions: Use the action buttons before sorting to earn bonus time.
Forgetting a required action (like Remove Lid) costs time!


⏱️ Tip: Correct sorts add time; wrong sorts remove it. Build combos for mega bonuses!


Based on NZ's 2023 national kerbside collection standards.
